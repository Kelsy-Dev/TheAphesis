What is The Gospel Foundation?

A Christian Organization that focuses on preaching the gospel to raise Christ-like disciples across nations through training, the use of media, literature, and works of love.

The Great Commission

Then Jesus came to them and said, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age (Matthew 28:18-20).

Our Vision

To see the next generation standing firm and mature in Christ, living a Spirit-filled life, and becoming a strong anchor for the next generations.

Our Mission

Preaching the gospel of Christ to the heart, to the culture, and to the mind - in love, in wisdom, and in power.

Our Comission

And the things you have heard me say in the presence of many witnesses entrust to reliable people who will also be qualified to teach others. For to me, to live is Christ and to die is gain (2 Timothy 2:2, Philippians 1:21)

Our Hope

In him was life, and that life was the light of all mankind. The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it (John 1:4-5)
